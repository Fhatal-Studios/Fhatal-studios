Client Agreement

Last updated: 18.5.2025

1. Scope of Work

The scope of work, deliverables, and timelines will be defined in a separate written proposal or email confirmation.

2. Payment Terms

Payment terms, including deposits and milestones, will be agreed upon before work begins. Full ownership transfers after payment.

3. Revisions

Reasonable revisions are included as specified in the proposal. Additional changes may incur extra charges.

4. Confidentiality

We treat all client information as confidential and do not share it with third parties except as required by law.

5. Termination

Either party may terminate the agreement with written notice. Work completed up to termination will be invoiced accordingly.
